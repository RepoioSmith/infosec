Q4 2024 Financial Review & Outlook — Internal Memorandum

From: Catherine Harris, CFO

To: Board of Directors, Executive Committee

Re: Q4 2024 Performance Summary / FY2025 Budget Projections

1. Executive Summary

NorthStar Bank & Trust closed Q4 2024 with net income of $47.3M, representing a 12.4% year-over-year increase. Total assets under management reached $8.9 billion, exceeding our FY2024 guidance of $8.5B. The digital banking migration completed in September contributed significantly to operational efficiency, reducing transaction processing costs by 18%.

3. Digital Banking Platform Update

The new customer portal (version 2.1.4) went live in November 2024. Adoption rates have exceeded projections, with 68% of consumer banking customers now using digital-first channels. The API integration layer supports corporate webhook callbacks for payment notifications — this feature is currently available to business accounts and will be rolled out to consumer accounts in Q1 2025 pending security review.

Note to IT Security: The webhook notification endpoint was flagged for review in our most recent internal audit (ref. NSB-SEC-2024-114). Pending resolution before Q2 2025 launch.

4. Risk & Compliance

Our annual penetration test (conducted by RedTeam Associates, October 2024) identified 3 medium and 1 low severity findings in the customer portal. All items are tracked in Jira under project NSB-SEC. Remediation deadline: Q1 2025.
